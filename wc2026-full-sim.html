📖 How The Simulation Works

▼
Full Tournament Simulation:
  • Ran 1,000 complete tournaments with 17 players each
  • Real WC 2026 groups from December 2025 draw
  • 72 group matches + 32 knockout matches (R32→R16→QF→SF→Final) = 104 total
  • Teams weighted by FIFA rankings + recent form
  • Home advantage for USA, Mexico, Canada (+0.05 rating boost)
  • Momentum/form: winning streaks give temporary boosts
  • Draw predictions enabled in group stage (24 possible draws)
  • Match outcomes favor higher-ranked teams significantly
  • Players with higher skill pick favorites more often
  • 5-team pick limit fully enforced in knockout rounds
  • Violation penalty: -2 lives if both teams at limit
  • Penalty shootout wins don't count toward limit
  • Score bonuses (+1 life) only in group stage
  • All players always make predictions (no missing penalties)
  • Only penalty: wrong prediction = -1 life
How Skill Level Works:
Group Stage Predictions:
  • High-skill players (0.8+) pick favorites ~80-90% of the time
  • Medium-skill players (0.5-0.7) pick favorites ~60-75% of the time
  • Low-skill players (0.2-0.4) pick almost randomly
  • All skill levels can predict draws for evenly-matched teams
Knockout Stage - Strategic Pick Management:
  • High-skill players strategically manage their 5-pick limit:
    • When a team reaches 4 picks, they consider saving the 5th pick
    • Factor in penalty shootout probability (penalties don't count toward limit)
    • For close matches with ~15% penalty chance, more willing to use limited team
    • For mismatches with ~5% penalty chance, avoid using limited team
    • Spread picks across multiple strong teams to avoid getting forced into bad picks
  • Low-skill players ignore the limit:
    • Keep picking the same favorites (Argentina, France, Brazil) repeatedly
    • Hit the 5-pick limit early in knockout rounds
    • Get forced to pick underdogs or skip matches (-2 lives)
    • Much higher elimination rate due to poor pick management
Why This Matters:
Skill level affects TWO things: (1) Picking the right team to win, and (2) Managing team picks strategically to avoid the 5-pick limit. High-skill players survive at much higher rates because they avoid forced bad picks.
