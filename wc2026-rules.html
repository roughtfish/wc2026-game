⚽Overview
🎮
Kamikaze Football is a survival prediction game. Every player starts with the same number of lives. Get a prediction wrong and you lose lives. Last one standing wins.
👑
One player is the Host. The host controls the game — starting rounds, entering results, and advancing to the next match. Everyone else joins as a player.
🔗
Players join on their own device using the 4-letter game code. They need to set a password when joining — this is required to rejoin if they close the page. ⚠️ Forgotten passwords cannot be reset.
📋Group Stage Predictions
✏️
Before the group stage begins, every player submits predictions for all 72 group stage matches at once — home win, away win, or draw.
💾
Predictions are auto-saved as you go. If you close the page and come back, your picks will be restored where you left off.
🔒
Once you click Lock in my predictions, your group stage picks are final.
⚡
The host can force start the group stage at any time. If you haven't locked in yet, you'll lose 2 lives per unpredicted match.
🏆Knockout Stage Predictions
🔄
Knockout predictions are submitted round by round. Before each new round begins, all players submit their picks for every match in that round at once.
❌
There are no draws in the knockout stage — you pick home win or away win only.
🎰
If your pick wins on penalties, that prediction is a free pick and doesn't count toward that team's 5-pick limit — so you can back them again next round.
⚡
The host can force start a round at any time. Players who haven't submitted predictions yet will automatically lose 2 lives per unpredicted match in that round.
⏳
The next round's prediction window opens automatically when the host advances past the last match of the current round.
❤️Scoring & Lives
✅
Correct prediction — Well done! No lives lost.
❌
Wrong prediction — you lose 1 life.
—
No prediction recorded — if you had no pick for a match (e.g. you were blocked by the pick limit or missed the prediction window), you lose 2 lives. This is double the penalty for a wrong prediction — always submit your picks before the host force-starts.
Example: You start with 40 lives. You get 3 wrong predictions in a row — you lose 3 lives and are down to 37. Get one right and nothing changes. Miss a prediction entirely (no pick submitted) and you lose 2 lives — down to 35.
⚠️The 5-Pick Limit
5️⃣
Each team can only be picked to win a maximum of 5 times across the entire tournament — group stage and all knockout rounds combined. Exception: if you predicted a team to win and they won on penalties, that prediction does not count toward your 5-pick limit for that team.
🚫
If a team has already been picked 5 times, the button for that team is disabled and greyed out. You cannot select them.
⚠️ Blocked pick penalty: If both teams in a match have reached the 5-pick limit, you cannot make a prediction for that match. You will automatically lose 2 lives when the result is entered — the same penalty as having no prediction recorded.
💡
Strategy tip: Spread your picks across different teams. Picking the same strong team (e.g. Brazil) too many times early in the group stage can lock you out of predicting their knockout matches.
=The 24-Draw Limit
=
You can predict a maximum of 24 draws across all 72 group stage matches. The draw button is disabled once you reach the limit.
💡
Group stage matches can end in a draw — but draws are more rare than wins. Spread your draw predictions wisely across the 72 matches.
❌
There are no draws in the knockout stage — every match must have a winner, so the draw option is not available for those predictions.
🎯Exact Score Bonus
🎯
For each group, you can optionally predict the exact score of one match in addition to your outcome pick. Tap the 🎯 +1 ❤️ Predict score button under any fixture after selecting your winner or draw. With 12 groups, you can make up to 12 exact score predictions in total.
❤️
If your exact score is correct, you gain 1 life — on top of your normal outcome result.
⚠️
The exact score bonus is independent of your outcome prediction. You can still lose a life for a wrong outcome even if you happened to guess the right score — though that would be very unlucky.
Example: You predict Brazil win and score 2–1 exactly. Brazil win 2–1. You get no lives lost (correct outcome) and gain 1 life (exact score) — net +1 life. Once you've used your score pick for Group C, the button is disabled for all other Group C matches.
❌
Exact score predictions are only available in the group stage. There is no score bonus in knockout rounds.
🥅Penalty Shootouts
⚽
In knockout matches that end level after 90 minutes, the host enters the full-time score and then selects the penalty shootout winner.
🎯
Predictions are judged on the overall winner — not just full time. If you predicted Brazil to win and they won on penalties, you get it right.
📊
Results shown as penalties are marked with (pens) on the stats page, along with an arrow showing which side prevailed.
🎰
Picking a team that goes on to win on penalties is a free pick — it does not count toward your 5-pick limit for that team. This lets you back a strong team again in the next round even if they squeaked through on spot kicks.
💀Elimination & Winning
💀
A player is eliminated when their lives reach zero. Eliminated players can still watch but no longer score or lose lives.
🏆
The game ends when only one player remains, or all matches are played. The player with the most lives remaining wins. Ties are broken by most correct predictions.
📱
The live stats page (📊 stats link in the header) shows standings, lives remaining, accuracy, and a full match-by-match prediction history for every player.
